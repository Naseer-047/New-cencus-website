News And Updates About Cencus 2025

The nationwide enumeration phase has officially started in all 28 states and 8 union territories, ensuring a fully digital census process.

Enumerators will now collect data through a secure government app, reducing manual errors and improving accuracy by 40%.

Over 10 lakh enumerators have completed digital training programs, ensuring uniform data entry across all districts.

India’s preliminary population is projected at 1.43 billion, with urbanization growing by 12% compared to 2011 census data.

All collected data is encrypted and securely stored in government cloud servers with strict access controls.

The Ministry of Statistics will release the first official Census 2025 insights report in early January next year.

The Department of NASS will release the first official Census App Will in End next year.
